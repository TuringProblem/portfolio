## Why Docusaurus?

After working with a custom portfolio setup, I decided to migrate to Docusaurus for several reasons:

1. **Built-in blog functionality** - Perfect for sharing thoughts and project updates
2. **MDX support** - I can embed React components directly in my blog posts
3. **SEO optimization** - Static site generation with excellent search engine optimization
4. **TypeScript support** - Full TypeScript integration out of the box
5. **Customization** - Still flexible enough to create custom components and layouts
